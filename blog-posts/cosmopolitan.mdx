# Cosmopolitan

<BlogTextWrapper>

Another fruity cocktail for Andreas.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 45ml vodka
- 22ml cranberry juice
- 22ml lime juice
- 22ml cointreau
- 10ml simple syrup

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds.

2. Strain into a chilled glass.

</BlogTextWrapper>
