# Basic vanilla ice cream

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1.5 dl sugar
- 3 dl full cream
- 2 dl milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Method

1. Beat the eggs lightly for 1-2 minutes.

2. Add the sugar a little at a time.

3. Add the cream and milk.

4. Pour into the ice cream maker and follow the manufacturer's instructions.

</BlogTextWrapper>
