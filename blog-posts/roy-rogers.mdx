# Roy Rogers

<BlogTextWrapper>

Another mocktail for the children who like something nice to drink with the grown ups. You could use a 50/50 mix of cola and Sprite, or you could add a splash of lemon or lime juice.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 200ml cola
- 10ml grenadine
- 1 maraschino cherry

### Method

1. Pour the cola into a highball glass and pour in the grenadine.

2. Add a maraschino cherry on top to serve.

</BlogTextWrapper>
