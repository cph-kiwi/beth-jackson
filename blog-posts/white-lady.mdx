# White Lady

<BlogTextWrapper>

If I have an egg white to use up, I make a White Lady.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 45ml gin
- 22ml cointreau
- 22ml lemon juice
- 7ml simple syrup
- 1 egg white

### Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker without ice and dry shake vigorously for 30 seconds to emulsify the egg white.

2. Add ice and shake again for 15-20 seconds.

3. Strain into a chilled glass.

</BlogTextWrapper>
