# Mudslide

<BlogTextWrapper>

I love a White Russian, but I don't have cream on hand very often. Now I've come to prefer this version.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 30ml vodka
- 30ml coffee liqueur
- 60ml Irish cream

### Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds.

2. Strain into a chilled glass.
