# French Martini

<BlogTextWrapper>

The sweet fruity cocktail for Andreas.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 45ml vodka
- 15ml chambord
- 30ml pineapple juice

### Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice and dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds.

2. Strain into a chilled glass.
