# Pizza sauce

<BlogTextWrapper>

Emergency dinner for children. Written down here just so that I can remember at what temp and for how long when I am sleep deprived.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- pita
- tomato puree
- cheese

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Method

1. Preheat oven to 220C, fan bake.

2. Spread tomato puree on pita. Scatter grated cheese on top. Place on baking paper on a tray.

3. Cook for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and pita is crispy.

</BlogTextWrapper>
