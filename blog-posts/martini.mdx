# Martini

<BlogTextWrapper>

This is precisely how I like my martinis, after a lot of trial and error.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 50ml gin
- 10ml dry vermouth
- 1/2 tsp olive brine
- olives to garnish

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice and stir for 15-20 seconds.

2. Strain into a chilled glass and add olives on a toothpick.

</BlogTextWrapper>
