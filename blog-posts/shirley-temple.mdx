# Shirley Temple

<BlogTextWrapper>

It's traditional to use the transparent kind of ginger ale, rather than cloudy ginger beer. You can make this with 100% ginger ale, and skip the Sprite. You could also add a splash of lemon or lime juice.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 100ml ginger ale
- 100ml Sprite
- 10ml grenadine
- 1 maraschino cherry

### Method

1. Pour the ginger ale and Sprite into a highball glass and pour in the grenadine.

2. Add a maraschino cherry on top to serve.

</BlogTextWrapper>
