# Lemon Drop

<BlogTextWrapper>

If I don't have what I need to make a White Lady, I make a Lemon Drop.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 60ml vodka
- 22ml lemon juice
- 7ml triple sec
- 15ml simple syrup

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Method

1. Combine all the ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice and dry shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds.

2. Strain into a chilled glass.

</BlogTextWrapper>
