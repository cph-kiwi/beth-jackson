# Rum hot toddy

<BlogTextWrapper>

This is my favourate way to have ginger wine.

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Ingredients

- 45ml dark rum
- 30ml ginger wine
- 1/2 tsp golden syrup
- juice of 1/2 a lime (20ml)
- 120ml hot water

</BlogTextWrapper>

<BlogTextWrapper>

### Method

1. Put all the ingredients except the water in a mug, stir and add the hot water.

2. Add more golden syrup or water to taste.

</BlogTextWrapper>
